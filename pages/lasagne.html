How to make Lasagne Bolognaise

Here are the ingredients you'll need

  • Minced beef
  • Red wine
  • Smoked paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Tinned pureed tomatoes
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Nutmeg
  • Lasagne sheets
  • Onion

Instructions

  1. Brown the mince in a pan
  2. Chop the onion and add to the mince
  3. When the onion is glassy, pour in the wine
  4. When the wine has reduced, add the tinned tomato puree and 1 tin of water
  5. Mix until water and tomato puree have combined
