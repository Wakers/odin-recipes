Egg Fried Rice

How to make Egg Fried Rice

Here are the ingredients you'll need

  • Caroline Rice
  • Chicken breast
  • Petite pois peas
  • Onion or spring onion
  • egg
  • Smoked paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Aromat
  • Soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Boil rice with the peas and leave to steam dry for 20-30 minutes
  2. Fry chicken breast with onion
  3. Crack egg into a different pan
  4. Make an omelette
  5. Add rice and peas, then soy sauce and mix on a high heat
