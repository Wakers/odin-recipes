Huerndli and Mince

How to make Huerndli and Mince

Here are the ingredients you'll need

  • Minced beef
  • Gravy or bratensauce
  • Smoked paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Huerndli or any other paste shape that isn't noodles
  • Carrot
  • Butter
  • Peas

Instructions

  1. Brown the mince in a pan
  2. Add water and gravy powder, stir well and allow to boil for 2 minutes
  3. Boil pasta in a pan with the peas
  4. WWhen the pasta is ready, drain and put back into the pan
  5. Pour mince and sauce into the pasta and mix
